We blend the freedom of the Montessori method with the security of a modern safety-first facility.

🧠

Child-Led Growth

Our Programs

Designed to support specific developmental stages with age-appropriate environments.

0 - 18 Months

Infant Community

A calm, home-like environment focusing on movement, language, and sensory exploration.

18 Months - 3 Years

Toddler House

Fostering independence in self-care, language explosion, and social grace and courtesy.

3 - 6 Years

Primary

Deep work in practical life, sensorial, language, mathematics, and cultural studies.

Safety is Our Foundation

At EdenSafe, we believe a child cannot learn freely unless they feel completely secure. Our facility features 24/7 monitoring, air purification systems, and strict visitor protocols.
